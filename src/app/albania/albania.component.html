Tavë Kosi
Let's Make It
Origins

Tavë kosi - soured milk casserole - is a national dish in Albania. It is a dish of lamb and rice baked with a mixture of yogurt and eggs added to a roux. It is also popular in Greece and Turkey, where it is known as Elbasan Tava, named after the Albanian city of Elbasan.

Ingredients
  • 2lb Cubed Lamb
  • 1/4 Cup Long White Rice
  • 2 Cups Water
    • 70g/2½oz butter 1 tbsp olive oil 1.2kg/2lb 12oz boned lamb shoulder, cut into 5cm/2in cubes 4 garlic cloves, grated 1 tsp dried oregano 60g/2¼oz long-grain rice, rinsed 50g/1¾oz plain flour 600ml/20fl oz Greek-style yoghurt 4 free-range eggs, beaten freshly grated nutmeg, to finish salt and freshly ground black pepper
Method
  • Clean the rice 4 times in a large bowl of cold water. Leave to soak while preparing the rest of the ingredients.
  • Make the Palaw Masala by grinding Corriander Seeds, Black Cardamom, Cumin Seeds, Anise, Cloves, Black Pepper and the Cinnamon stick in a coffee grinder until powdered. Put to one side.
  • Slice Carrot into julienne strips and finely dice the onion. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add Carrot, Sugar and Cardamom Powder and stir occasionally until slightly softened. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on a sheet of tin foil.
  • Add Raisins and Pine Nuts to oil and fry for 1 minute. Remove with a slotted spoon and add to Carrot. Wrap in a parcel and put to one side.
  • Add Onion to pan and fry until golden and transluscent. Add Lamb, Turmeric and 1 tbsp of Palaw Masala and fry until sealed. Add salt and water, bring to boil then lower to simmer until lamb is tender. Approximately 45 minutes.
  • Once Lamb is cooked remove from broth and put to one side. Sieve the broth into a large bowl. This will be used to cook the Rice.
  • Add Sugar to a large pot and heat until melted. Add broth to the pot and bring to the boil. Reduce heat to a simmer, drain water from Rice and add to pot. Cover and cook on low until Rice is 80% cooked.
  • Sieve Rice from the broth and layer into a caserole dish, sprinkling the rest of Palaw Masala between layers. Ladel the remaining broth over the Rice. Place Lamb on top of the Rice on one side and the parcel of Carrots, Raisins and Pine Nuts on the other side. Cover and cook in the oven at 350 farenheit for 15 minutes.
  • Remove parcel and stir Lamb into the Rice. Cover the dish with Carrots, Raisins, Pine Nuts and serve.
