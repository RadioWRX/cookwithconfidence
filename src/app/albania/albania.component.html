Ingredients
  • 2lb Cubed Lamb
  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 4 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • 1 Medium Sweet Onion
  • 2 tbsp Pine Nuts
  • Â½ Cup Raisins
  • 3 tbsp White Sugar
  • 1 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 3 Black Cardamom Pods
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp Anise
  • 1 tsp Cloves
  • 1 tsp Corriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric
Method
  1. Clean the rice 4 times in a large bowl of cold water. Leave to soak while you prepare the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Prepare the Palaw Masala by grinding Corriander Seeds, Black Cardamom, Cumin Seeds, Anise, Cloves, Black Pepper and Cinnamon stick in a coffee grinder until powdered. Put to one side.
  3. Slice Carrot into julienne strips and finely dice the onion. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add Carrot, Sugar and Cardamom Powder and stir occasionally until slightly softened. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on a sheet of tin foil.
  4. Add Raisins and Pine Nuts to oil and fry for 1 minute. Remove with a slotted spoon and add to Carrot. Wrap in a parcel and put to one side.
  5. Add Onion to pan and fry until golden and transluscent. Add Lamb, Turmeric and 1 tbsp of Palaw Masala and fry until sealed. Add salt and water and bring to boil then lower to simmer until lamb is soft.
  6. Once Lamb is cooked remove from broth and put to one side. Sieve the broth into a large bowl. This will be used to cook the Rice.
  7. Add Sugar to a large pot and heat until melted. Add broth to the pot and bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low, drain water from Rice and add to pan. Cover and cook on low until Rice is 80% cooked.
  8. Sieve Rice from the broth and layer into a caserole dish, sprinkling the rest of Palaw Masala between layers. Ladel the remaining broth over the Rice. Place Lamb on top of the Rice one side and the parcel of Carrots, Raisins and Pine Nuts on the other side. Cover and cook in the oven at 350 farenheit for 15 minutes.
  9. Remove parcel and stir Lamb into the Rice. Cover the dish with Carrots, Raisins, Pine Nuts and serve.
